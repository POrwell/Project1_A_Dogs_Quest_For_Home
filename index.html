A Dog's Quest for Home

How to Play

Help navigate Blue from her life in an abandoned forest to a warm home with a loving family. Collect resources to help keep her strength up or collect points, but be sure to dodge any danger as you go, or she may lose too much strength and have to begin her quest again!

  • move Blue around using the arrow keys
  • collect resources using Enter to build strength and win points
  • dodge dangers to avoid a drop in strength
